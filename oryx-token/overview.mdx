### Burn Mechanism

A portion of revenue from premium features is used to buy back and burn $TERRA tokens:

- 20% of premium subscription fees
- 15% of MCP marketplace fees
- 10% of priority execution fees

This creates deflationary pressure and rewards long-term holders.
